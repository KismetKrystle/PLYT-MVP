# PLYT - Eat & Grow
**Connecting Farmers & Consumers through AI-Driven Local Food Systems.**

PLYT is a platform designed to make local food systems accessible, sustainable, and transparent. It empowers users to find local produce, start their own hydroponic systems, and track their social and environmental impact.

The best way to experience PLYT is to flow from a curious guest to an empowered grower.

### 1. **The Guest Experience (Chat Interface)**
-   **Start here**: The landing page is an AI Chat Interface.
-   **Try asking**:
    -   "How do I start growing tomatoes?"
    -   "Where can I buy organic kale in Bali?"
-   **Action**: The AI will guide you and eventually prompt you to **Sign In** to access more tools.

### 2. **The User Dashboard**
-   **Sign In**: You can use test credentials `test@example.com` / `password` (or Sign Up freely).
-   **Main Chat Hub**: This is your control center.
    -   **Find Produce Tab**: Chat to find food. Watch the **Right Panel** dynamically populate with items and costs.
    -   **Pick a System Tab**: Plan your own farm. The **Right Panel** builds your setup (Systems + Containers) with cost calculation.
    -   **Learn Tab**: Ask questions and save them as Note/Lessons.

### 3. **Deep Dive Features (Left Sidebar)**
Navigate through the global sidebar to see the full ecosystem:
-   **Your Systems**: View a live dashboard of your connected hydroponic towers (Status, Stats, Gallery).
-   **Impact Score**: See your visualized social and climate impact based on your actions.
-   **Store**: Browse the marketplace for systems and supplies.
-   **Orders**: Track your purchase history across food, products, and services.
